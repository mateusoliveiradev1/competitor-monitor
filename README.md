# 🕵️♂️ Competitor Monitor Agent
Never get blindsided by a competitor's pricing change or pivot again. This autonomous intelligence agent scans competitor websites, extracts their latest positioning, and contrasts it directly against your own business baseline to generate immediate, actionable counter-strategies.

### ✨ Enterprise Features
* **Baseline Contrast:** Compares their new data directly against your current setup.
* **MECE Analysis:** Delivers Mutually Exclusive and Collectively Exhaustive insights.
* **Counter-Attack SOPs:** Gives your sales team a Standard Operating Procedure to combat the competitor's new angles.
